SIMMONS

J.K.

J.K. Simmons (born January 9, 1955) is an American actor. One of the most prolific character actors of his generation, he has appeared in over 200 film and TV roles since his debut in 1986. He is the recipient of various accolades, including an Academy Award, a British Academy Film Award and a Golden Globe Award.
